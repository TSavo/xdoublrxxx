About Doublr

In order to interact with the Doublr smart contract, you need to have MetaMask installed. Click on the "Installation video" button to see how to install MetaMask. Click on "Download MetaMask" to download and install MetaMask.

How to fund your Metamask account
Open MetaMask, click the '...' icon, and select 'Copy address to clipboard'.
Go to where you currently have ETH (Binance, Gemini, etc.) and find the 'Send' or 'Transfer' feature for your wallet.
Transfer Ethereum to your MetaMask address.
